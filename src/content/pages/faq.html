FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We've compiled answers to frequently asked questions about roof replacement, repair options, and pricing structures—everything you need to know before investing in your home's protection.

  • A new roof in Edmonds, WA typically costs $10,000–$16,000 for a standard asphalt-shingle roof on an average-sized home, with simpler jobs sometimes under $10,000 and more complex or premium-material roofs exceeding $20,000. Prices vary mainly based on roof size, complexity, materials, and any needed structural repairs. Overall, Edmonds’ proximity to the Seattle area also pushes labor and permitting costs slightly higher.

  • Most residential roof replacements in Edmonds are completed in 1-3 days, weather permitting. Our experienced crews work efficiently without cutting corners—we'll provide you with a precise timeline during your consultation based on your roof's size and complexity. We protect your landscaping, maintain a clean job site daily, and use a magnetic sweep to ensure no nails are left behind. For emergency repairs, we offer same-day response to prevent further damage.

  • Standard homeowners insurance in Washington (including around Seattle) typically does cover roof damage if it was caused by a “covered peril,” such as a windstorm, hail, lightning, falling objects (like a tree branch), fire, or heavy ice/snow load. On the other hand, damage from normal wear and tear, slow leaks, lack of maintenance, or age-related deterioration is usually not covered.

  • You can prevent moss growth on your roof by keeping it clean, and well-maintained. Trim back overhanging branches to reduce shade and improve airflow, then regularly clear debris like leaves and needles that trap moisture. For long-term prevention, consider installing zinc or copper strips along the roof ridge, as rainwater activates these metals and naturally inhibits moss growth.

  • All our roofs come with a full manufacturer warranty, ensuring your materials are protected directly by the producer. This coverage gives you long-term peace of mind, knowing any defects in the roofing products are backed by the manufacturer. Combined with our professional installation, you receive a durable, reliable roof supported by strong industry-standard protection.
