Roofing Materials We Install

The right roofing material depends on multiple factors including your budget, architectural style, performance requirements, and maintenance preferences. We install various roofing systems, each offering distinct advantages.

Asphalt Shingle Roofing

Asphalt shingles remain the most popular residential roofing choice, offering excellent value, proven performance, and wide-ranging style options. We install architectural shingles that provide superior weather resistance and enhanced curb appeal compared to basic three-tab shingles.

Metal Roofing Systems

Metal roofing delivers exceptional longevity, energy efficiency, and minimal maintenance requirements. Available in standing seam, metal shingles, or panel systems, metal roofing suits properties where long-term performance and distinctive appearance are priorities.

Specialty Roofing Materials

From composite materials mimicking slate or cedar to tile roofing for Mediterranean styles, we install specialty materials that match specific architectural requirements while providing modern performance benefits.

Our New Roof Installation Process

Successful roof installation requires careful planning and systematic execution. We follow proven procedures that ensure your new roof delivers optimal performance from day one.

Comprehensive Consultation and Planning

We begin by understanding your goals, evaluating your property, discussing material options, and developing detailed plans that address all aspects of your new roof installation. This thorough planning phase ensures the finished result meets your expectations.

Precise Measurements and Material Ordering

Accurate measurements ensure proper material quantities while minimizing waste. We order materials to arrive precisely when needed, maintaining project schedules without unnecessary delays.

Proper Preparation and Protection

Before installation begins, we prepare your property by protecting landscaping, securing the work area, and ensuring safe access for our installation crews. This preparation prevents damage and enables efficient work.

Expert Installation Following Best Practices

Our experienced installation crews follow manufacturer specifications and industry best practices for every component of your roofing system. From underlayment to final shingles, attention to installation details ensures long-term performance.

Thorough Cleanup and Final Inspection

Once installation is complete, we conduct comprehensive cleanup including magnetic sweeps for nails and debris removal. A final inspection verifies that every aspect of your new roof meets our quality standards.
